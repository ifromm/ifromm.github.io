Quantum and Geometry IR

I created a Mendeley group on the topic of geometric and quantum-inspried information retrieval approaches. Here I collect all publications that are relevant to this area and that I came across. The idea is to create an overview of all relevant literature in this emerging field. On the long run I regard this group as a community effort. You may follow this group on Mendeley; if you're a researcher in the field of quantum and geometry IR, please feel free to contact me on Mendeley and join this group if you want to contribute here as well.
